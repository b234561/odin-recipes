Pasta Pesto

Description

Pasta Pesto is a simple pasta recipe, with plenty of room for improvisation in the content.

This recipe oly contains the base recipe for the pesto and pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Fresh basil
  • Garlic
  • Grana padano or Parmiggiano Reggiano.
  • Sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds or something similar.
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a large pan on low heat, fry the seeds until aromatic and remove them and wait until cooled.
  2. In a large pot, bring the pasta water to a boil.
  3. Prepare the Basil by putting the leaves in the blender.
  4. Add the garlic, cheese and oil to the blender and blend them. Add the seeds and continue blending until desired texture.
  5. Once the water is boiling, add a bunch of salt to the water and add the pasta. Boil according to the package instructions, but subtract a few minutes, as the pasta will cook on in the pan later.
  6. Add the pasta to the pan along with a ladle of pasta water, add the pesto and stir until creamy.
  7. Serve the dish. Bon appetit.
